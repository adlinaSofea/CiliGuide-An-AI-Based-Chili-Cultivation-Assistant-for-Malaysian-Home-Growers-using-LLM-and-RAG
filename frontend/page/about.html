About CiliGuide

Discover how CiliGuide simplifies your chili farming journey.

CiliGuide is an AI-powered cultivation assistant designed to support Malaysian home chili growers with reliable, practical, and easy-to-understand guidance. By combining agricultural knowledge with advanced artificial intelligence, the system helps users make informed decisions throughout the entire cultivation process.

Why Built CiliGuide

CiliGuide was developed to address the challenges faced by home growers, such as inconsistent information, difficulty diagnosing plant issues, and uncertainty in fertilizer and care practices. The system aims to provide accessible and trustworthy guidance that supports healthier crops and more confident growers.

What the System Offers

The platform provides key AI-driven features:

  • Chili Advisory Chat
  • Fertilizer And Basic Care Guidance
  • Soil Analysis
  • Disease Detection (Text-based)
  • Post-Harvest Suggestions

Academic Contribution & Innovation

CiliGuide was developed as a Final Year Project to demonstrate the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Vision–Language Models (VLMs) within a web-based system for the agricultural domain. The project highlights the potential of intelligent AI-driven tools to support local food production, digital transformation, and sustainable home agriculture in Malaysia.
