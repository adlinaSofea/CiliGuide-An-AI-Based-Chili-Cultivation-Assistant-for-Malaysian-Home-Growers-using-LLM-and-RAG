Everything you need to know about CiliGuide

CiliGuide is an AI-powered platform designed to help Malaysian home growers and farmers cultivate healthier chili plants. It uses LLM and RAG technology to provide personalised advice on fertilisation, disease detection, soil analysis and harvest tracking.
Yes! CiliGuide is completely free for all registered users. Simply create an account and you'll have full access to all features including Chat Advisory, Disease Detection, Harvest Tracker and more.
The Chat Advisory uses a large language model combined with a curated chili knowledge base. Just type your question about watering, pests, fertiliser timing or anything else and get an accurate, context-aware answer instantly.
Disease Detection uses text-based input to help identify common chili plant diseases and deficiencies. Simply describe your plant's symptoms — such as leaf colour changes, spots, wilting or unusual growth — and CiliGuide will analyse your description and suggest the most likely issue along with treatment recommendations.
CiliGuide supports popular Malaysian varieties including Cili Padi, Cili Benggala, Cili Besar. Each variety has specific growth timelines and care recommendations built in.
